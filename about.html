About the Study

ABOUT US

This study aims to find out whether using technology can benefit families in supporting and encouraging children to stay active and healthy. He or she can be between the ages of 6 and 10 and is currently enrolled in a participating YMCA afterschoool program in Metro Atlanta. What is learned from this research has the potential for helping other children and families in the future. Your child may experience an increase in physical activity. Your child may also learn about the health benefits of staying physically active and fit via the virtual dog.This study is funded by the National Institutes of Health.
