Can technology help children become more physically active?

Learn More!

Welcome to The Virtual Fitness Buddy Ecosystem

The VFB ecosystem uses digital technology to promote and sustain moderate to vigorous intensive physical activity in children ages 6 to 10 in Metro-Atlanta YMCA afterschool programs.

Fitbit

Each child will receive a Fitbit activity monitor to wear on their wrist for a year. At the conclusion, all participants will have the opportunity to keep their Fitbits.

Personalized

Primary guardians involved in the study will receive a link to a personalized website with their child's physical activity and sleep data.

As a Thank You

Each participating family will recieve a montetary incentive for completing surveys throughout the study.
